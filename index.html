Tutorial

Welcome to the Personalized Budget Planner tutorial! Follow these steps to make the most out of the planner:

  1. Create an Account: Click on the "Sign Up" button and fill in your details to create an account. If you already have an account, log in using the "Login" button.
  2. Set Up Your Profile: After logging in, go to the "Profile" section to update your personal information and upload a profile picture.
  3. Track Your Income and Expenses: Use the "Budget Manager" to add and manage your income and expenses. Provide a description, amount, date, and category for each transaction.
  4. Set Financial Goals: In the "Budget Manager" section, set your financial goals to help you stay on track with your savings and spending.
  5. Split Bills: Use the "Bill Splitter" feature to split expenses with friends and family. Enter the number of participants and their respective expenses to calculate the split.
  6. Scan Receipts: Easily scan and manage your receipts using the "Scan Receipt" feature. Upload a photo of your receipt and the system will extract the relevant information.
  7. Get Insights: Go to the "Insights" section to view personalized financial insights based on your income and expense data. The dashboard provides visualizations of your spending trends, budget forecasts, and financial health.
  8. Chat with Our Bot: If you have any questions or need financial advice, use the "Chatbot" feature to get instant responses.
  9. Provide Feedback: We value your feedback! Use the "Feedback" section to share your thoughts and suggestions for improving the planner.

If you need further assistance, feel free to contact us through the "Contact" link in the footer.
